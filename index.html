Calendars

The following links can be clicked to do a one-time import of events into your calendar.

  • Kuei Luck Calendar (https://tinyurl.com/kueiluck-calendar)

Alternatively, you can subscribe to the calendar to receive future updates by following these steps:

iPhone

  1. Open Calendar
  2. Click "Calendars" at the bottom
  3. Click "Add Calendar"
  4. Click "Add Subscription Calendar"
  5. Enter the URL from above, e.g. "https://tinyurl.com/kueiluck-calendar"
  6. Click "Subscribe"

Android

  1. Open Google Calendar
  2. Next to "Other calendars", click "+"
  3. Click "From URL"
  4. Enter the URL from above, e.g. "https://tinyurl.com/kueiluck-calendar"
  5. Click "Add calendar"
